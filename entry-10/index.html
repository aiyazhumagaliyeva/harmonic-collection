What is Lower East Side?

The Lower East Side, sometimes abbreviated as LES, is a historic neighborhood in the southeastern part of Manhattan in New York City. Traditionally an immigrant, working class neighborhood, it began rapid gentrification in the mid-2000s, prompting the National Trust for Historic Preservation to place the neighborhood on their list of America's Most Endangered Places in 2008.

What is IN Lower East Side?

Economy Candy is a candy store in Lower East Side. This Lower East Side sweets emporium was founded in 1937 and continues to be run by the same family. Famous for its selection of long-forgotten brands of candy—Mary Jane, Mallo Cup, Black Jack—as well as novelties, chocolate (both imported and domestic) and more, this is a true candy land come to life. located in 108 Rivington St, Manhattan, NY, 10002
