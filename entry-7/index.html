ASTORIA

Astoria is a neighborhood in the western portion of the New York City borough of Queens. Astoria is bounded by the East River and is adjacent to four other Queens neighborhoods: Long Island City to the southwest, Sunnyside to the southeast, and Woodside and East Elmhurst to the east. As of 2019, Astoria has an estimated population of 95,446.

                             

I went to astoria on November 4, 2024, and it took me around 1 hr to get there from Williamsburg. Average commute from Union Square Park should be 30 minutes. The best trains to take are M,R,N,W.

Astoria is a very cute and pretty place with a lot of good food, a pretty park, and cozy architecture. As far as I know astoria has some interesting museums and music venues worth visiting, that i was not able to go to yet, but will be coming back for them! The park is very cute with an amazing view!

The main reason for my visit to Astoria was that I am going to a concert of a russian rock band there on November 22nd, and wanted to see what the area is like before I actually go. Another bonus of going was that I have a friend who lives there and promised her to come over a lot time a go, so it was both useful and a nice way to reunite with a friend!
