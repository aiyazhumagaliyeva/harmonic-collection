Dumbo

What is Dumbo known for?

  Dumbo, an acronym for Down Under the Manhattan Bridge Overpass.
  It is one of Brooklyn's most visited neighborhoods, 
  Known for cobblestone streets, dramatic architecture, great dining and dreamy views across the river.
